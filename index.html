Who We Are

The Catalyst for a Nature-Positive Future

Canopeia is a leading environmental consultancy founded on the conviction that healthy ecosystems are the foundation of sustainable economies. We are a global team of ecologists, social scientists, economists and policy specialists committed to bridging science and practice.

  • 🔬
    Scientific Integrity
    All our work is grounded in peer-reviewed science and transparent methodologies.
  • 🤝
    Inclusive Partnerships
    We co-design solutions with local communities, governments, and the private sector.
  • 🌍
    Systems Thinking
    We address biodiversity, climate, and society as interconnected, not isolated, challenges.
  • 📐
    Accountability
    We set measurable targets and report honestly on outcomes and lessons learned.
